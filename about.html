About Me

I grew up in a medium income house. Had my first Desktop computer when i was in secondary and i fell inlove with how it was able to perform a lot of tasks. I am talking about tasks like editing a document, presenation, taking photo as well as videos, playing music and storing files in folders. After that experience of using the computer at home i realized that i saw that i can also become a coder. I studied in a science and mathematics secondary called Mbilwi Secondary School in Sibasa. I matriculated in 2007 and went to University of Pretoria(Tuks) to study B.Eng Electronic Engineering influenced by my father who is an electrician. During my stay at Tuks, i managed to research about a coding degree and i found they offer Bsc Computer science which i liked but was unable to change immediately hence i deregistered engineering. I came back in 2016 at University of Limpopo where i did Bsc Mathematical sciences and graduated in 2019. I tried to major in subjects that relate to Bsc Computer Science awhich where Computer Science and Statistics. After that i spent 18 months training to be a Data Scientist first with Deviare and then Explore Data Science Academy. I was not successful finding a job in Data Science and hence I am currently doing training to become a Software Developer with The Digital Academy.
